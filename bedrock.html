How to connect to The Tribe Network

First launch minecraft and copy the following IP Address: thetribe.ppg.one


Next enter the Minecraft multiplayer menu

In the multiplayer menu click "add server"

Next type any name into the server name slot and paste the address you copied into the address box.

now type "19135" in the Port box


Press the "Save" button and the server will be on your servers list.

Joining a game

Once you connect you will see spawn and find portals, walk into the portal of your choice to join your desired world


Congrats on joining and welcome to the server!
